TSA stands for technological student association and is a national organization that creates leaders in STEM. Every year, TSA holds a competition that consists of regional, state and national conferences where students can compete in team or individual events. The events range from a series of topics from architecture to coding. TSA is recognized worldwide and the organization has chapters in almost every state. TSA works to create our future leaders in STEM not only in high school, but also elementary, middle school and in college.

PA TSA Region 3 Conference

On February 4th, 2023, Cumberland Valley hosted the 2023 PA TSA Region 3 conference at Mountain View Middle School. We are so proud to have received the opportunity to hold a event for students of all ages to come and display their acheivements. The event was managed by Jason Kofmehl, CV TSA's chapter advisor, and the CV TSA officer team. Cumberland Valley hopes to continue our support TSA in the future.
