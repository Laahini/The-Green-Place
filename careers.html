Highly motivated and values-driven, we’re working hard to make the world a more sustainable place.


Who we are

We are a community united by a shared vision for a greener, healthier planet. As advocates for mindful living, we have carefully curated a collection of resources that reflect our commitment to sustainability. We believe that each one of us plays a pivotal role in the delicate balance of nature, and our choices should reflect a deep respect for the environment. We are stewards of the planet. We are advocates for change. We are a community of purpose. We are The Green Place.

What we do

We believe that it should not be hard for homeowners to make positive change. We want you to be successful in making a positive impact on the environment and help others do the same! In addition to providing resources, our service also provides a collection of volunteer opportunites posted by users of The Green Place for other people to get involved! No matter where you are from, or what’s growing in your garden, everyone deserves to enjoy a sustainable future.

Our mission

At The Green Place, we strive to help homeowners pave the way for a sustainable future, where conscious choices are not just a preference but a way of life. The Green Place is a platform for positive change, where every choice matters. Join us and our community in creating a world where conscious decisions lead to a brighter, more sustainable future.
