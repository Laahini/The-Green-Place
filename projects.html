Discover Clean and Green


Solar Panels

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, reducing dependence on traditional power sources. Estimated Cost: $15,000 - $25,000 (before incentives)

Geothermal Heat Pumps

Utilizes the stable temperature of the earth to heat and cool homes efficiently. Estimated Cost: $10,000 - $30,000

Energy-Efficient Windows

Improves insulation and reduces heat transfer, lowering heating and cooling costs. Estimated Cost: $8,000 - $20,000 (for a whole house)

Smart Thermostats

Allows homeowners to optimize heating and cooling based on usage patterns, saving energy. Estimated Cost: $200 - $300

LED Lighting

Replacing traditional bulbs with energy-efficient LEDs reduces electricity consumption. Estimated Cost: $2 - $10 per bulb

Rainwater Harvesting System

Collects and stores rainwater for non-potable uses like watering plants and flushing toilets. Estimated Cost: $1,000 - $5,000

Solar Water Heaters

Uses sunlight to heat water, reducing the energy needed for hot water in homes. Estimated Cost: $1,500 - $5,000

Wind Turbines

Small-scale wind turbines harness wind energy to generate electricity for homes. Estimated Cost: $10,000 - $70,000 (before incentives)

Energy-Efficient Appliances

Upgrading to appliances with high Energy Star ratings reduces overall energy consumption. Estimated Cost: Varies per appliance, but could range from $500 - $3,000
